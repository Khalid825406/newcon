Rental Equipment

We specialize in renting and sourcing heavy-duty vehicles specifically designed for construction and earthwork operations.

We cater to a diverse range of clients, from industrial to personal needs. Our offerings include the supply, service, and rental of various equipment, such as materials handling, welding, construction tools, mobile cranes, compressors, boom trucks, and other industrial equipment and services.

Boom Lift

Boom Lifts are essential for reaching elevated work areas safely and efficiently. Whether you're working on high-rise buildings, installing lights, repairing facades, or managing warehouse operations, our Boom Lifts offer exceptional height access, maneuverability, and stability. Designed with powerful hydraulics and extendable arms, these lifts ensure maximum reach with minimal effort. Rent our Boom Lifts for a safe, productive, and cost-effective solution to your vertical access needs.

Bulldozers

Bulldozers are powerful tracked machines built for heavy-duty earthmoving, land clearing, and grading. With a large front blade and robust engine power, they are ideal for leveling ground, pushing soil, or clearing debris on construction and mining sites. Our rental bulldozers ensure maximum efficiency and productivity in even the most rugged terrains. Choose from various models to suit your project needs and timelines.

Excavators

Excavators are versatile and powerful machines used for digging, trenching, demolition, and material handling. Equipped with a hydraulic arm, rotating cab, and robust tracks, they provide excellent precision and performance on all types of terrains. Our excavator rentals are perfect for construction, landscaping, and large-scale excavation projects. Choose from compact to large models depending on your site requirements.

Skid Steer Loaders

Skid Steer Loaders are compact, agile, and powerful machines ideal for digging, lifting, and material handling in tight spaces. Their small frame and precise maneuverability make them perfect for urban construction, landscaping, and renovation work. With a wide range of attachments available, these machines are a multi-functional solution for a variety of job site needs. Rent our Skid Steer Loaders for unmatched versatility and performance.

Backhoe Loaders

Backhoe Loaders combine the capabilities of a loader and an excavator in one powerful machine. They are widely used for digging, trenching, backfilling, and material handling on construction and agricultural sites. With a front bucket and a rear digging arm, these machines offer excellent flexibility and productivity. Our Backhoe Loaders are ideal for small to medium-sized projects where multitasking and mobility are key.

Scissor Lifts

Scissor Lifts are ideal for vertical elevation in indoor and outdoor job sites where safety and stability are critical. Perfect for maintenance, installation, and construction work, these lifts provide a secure platform for workers to reach various heights. Our rental scissor lifts come in both electric and diesel models, ensuring you have the right solution for your specific work environment.

Compactors

Compactors are essential for soil and asphalt compaction in road construction, building foundations, and landscaping projects. These machines ensure a stable and level surface by removing air pockets and increasing soil density. Our rental compactors are available in different types including plate, roller, and rammer compactors to suit various job site conditions. Rent high-performance compactors to ensure long-lasting, solid groundwork.

Manlifts

Manlifts are designed to lift personnel safely to elevated work areas for tasks such as maintenance, inspection, and installation. Compact and easy to maneuver, these machines are ideal for use in warehouses, factories, construction sites, and indoor facilities. Our rental manlifts ensure stability, safety, and efficient vertical access, making them a perfect choice for jobs that require height with precision.

Dump Trucks

Dump Trucks are essential for transporting loose materials such as sand, gravel, demolition waste, and construction debris. Built for durability and heavy-duty hauling, these trucks are ideal for construction, mining, and roadwork operations. Our rental fleet includes a range of dump trucks with various capacities to suit projects of all sizes. Ensure efficient and safe material handling with our reliable dump truck rentals.

Dump Rollers

Dump Rollers are powerful compaction machines used to flatten and compress soil, gravel, or asphalt in construction and roadwork projects. These heavy-duty rollers ensure a stable and even surface, which is essential for building strong foundations and long-lasting roads. Our rental dump rollers are available in various sizes and weights to meet specific project requirements, offering optimal performance and reliability on every terrain.

Wheel Loaders

Wheel Loaders are versatile heavy-duty machines used for moving, loading, and transporting materials such as gravel, soil, sand, and debris on construction sites. With powerful engines and durable tires, they offer excellent mobility, stability, and load capacity even on rough terrain. Our rental wheel loaders are perfect for site preparation, material handling, and earthmoving tasks, ensuring fast and efficient project execution.

Forklifts

Forklifts are essential material-handling machines used in warehouses, factories, and construction sites for lifting and transporting heavy loads. Designed for precision and stability, they are ideal for stacking pallets, loading trucks, and organizing materials efficiently. Our forklift rentals include various models — electric, diesel, and LPG — to suit indoor and outdoor applications. Enhance your operational productivity with our safe and powerful forklift solutions.

Pickups

Pickups are compact yet powerful vehicles ideal for transporting tools, materials, and light equipment across construction sites or urban locations. Known for their speed, flexibility, and easy maneuverability, they’re perfect for quick deliveries, inter-site movement, and small-scale logistics. Our rental pickups are available in multiple configurations to match your project needs — offering convenience and cost-efficiency every step of the way.

Coasters

Coasters are comfortable and spacious mini-buses commonly used for transporting construction crews, engineers, and staff between job sites. With seating capacity ranging from 17 to 30 passengers, they offer a reliable and efficient solution for workforce mobility. Our rental coasters come equipped with air conditioning, comfortable seating, and safety features — ensuring your team travels in comfort and on time.

Buses

Buses are the ideal solution for transporting large groups of workers, engineers, or site staff to and from construction locations efficiently. With high seating capacity, enhanced safety features, and comfortable interiors, our rental buses ensure reliable and timely workforce movement across job sites. Choose from a range of AC and non-AC models based on your site and travel requirements to keep your team moving with ease.
