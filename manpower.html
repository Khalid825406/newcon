Manpower Supplies

We connect right talent with the right opportunities.

ABSO Manpower Services is a top-rated manpower supply company in Saudi Arabia, specializing in recruitment services, personalized HR staffing, payroll outsourcing, and local labor solutions. We cater to leading companies across various sectors, including oil and gas, engineering and construction, medical and healthcare, and hospitality.

Whether you need skilled labor, contract labor, temporary staffing, or employee leasing, ABSO Manpower provides reliable, high-quality, and professional labor supply services, backed by over years of industry experience and a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Being one of the best manpower supply companies in Saudi Arabia, we provide cost-effective and efficient manpower staffing solutions and HR outsourcing to all KSA companies looking to optimize their workforce.

Engineering and Construction (CIVIL & MEP)

ABSO Manpower boasts a vast database of skilled construction workers and maintains strong relationships with top construction companies in Saudi Arabia. We specialize in recruiting masons, plumbers, steel fixers, electricians, foremen, and laborers.

Get in touch with us to build your ideal construction workforce!

Oil and Gas

ABSO Manpower is renowned for supplying the best workers to leading oil and gas companies in Saudi Arabia. Our expertise includes recruiting riggers, drillers, welders, fabricators, and more. We also offer plant shutdown and turnaround management services.

Connect with us today to build your oil and gas team exactly how you envision it.

Medical and Healthcare

ABSO Manpower is a leading provider of medical staffing solutions in Saudi Arabia. We specialize in delivering professional healthcare staff, including physicians, nurses, lab technicians, and janitors, to top hospitals, clinics, and medical facilities across the region.

Let’s collaborate to achieve success in your healthcare staffing needs!

Hospitality

Whether it's staffing for hospitality businesses or placing skilled workers in catering roles, ABSO Manpower has successfully placed thousands of professionals in Saudi Arabia's top hotels, restaurants, resorts, and cafes.

Let’s work together to provide you with top-notch recruitment solutions!
